A mini game written in Processing. The cat exists 12 forms. There are 4 backgrounds, one for startScreen, one for gameOverScreem, and two for the adventure, in which you will see different scenes when you overcome difficulties(obstacles). There are also some comments occurring on the screen when you get different scores. Waiting for you to explore it! Moreover, you can adjust the parameters in the program to change the difficulty of the game (I have added comments on related variables in the program).).

UTM STUDENT SIMULATOR

A TEXT PLOT GAME written in Twine. "U of T Student: Life Simulation" is a game that simulates college student life. You are going to play the role of a current second-year student at UTM, and your major is CCIT. Your goal is to complete the 2023 Fall semester and get a grade and GPA as good as possible. At the same time, you also need to pay attention to your mental health. Please take care of your own health while studying. During school time, you will need to keep a positive mindset to face all kinds of challenges and opportunities. The direction of the game is determined by the player's choice. It is worth noting that this game has 9 different endings.
